Geography Lessons

Maps, landforms, regions, communities, and place-based reasoning.

  • Maps & Globes

    Students learn how maps and globes represent Earth, how symbols and legends help readers understand places, and how cardinal directions make routes easier to explain.

  • Landforms & Bodies of Water

    Students identify common landforms and bodies of water, compare features such as mountains and valleys, and explain how coasts and islands shape human choices.

  • Regions & Communities

    Students learn to describe regions by physical and human features, connect climate and resources to daily life, and plan routes or communities using geography evidence.
